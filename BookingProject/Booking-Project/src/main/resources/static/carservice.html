Car Servies List

Book a Mechanic. Maintain Your Vehicle. Drive with Confidence!

Engine Oil and Filter

Check and replace the engine oil and oil filter to ensure proper lubrication and engine health.

Tires

Inspect tire tread wear, check tire pressure, and rotate tires for even wear.

Brakes

Inspect the brake pads and discs for wear and check the brake fluid level.

Lights and Wipers

Test all exterior and interior lights, and inspect and replace windshield wiper blades as needed.

Air Filters

Check the engine air filter and cabin air filter for dirt and replace them to ensure good air quality and performance.

Battery

Test the battery's strength and check terminals for corrosion.

Suspension and Steering

Inspect suspension components like shocks/struts and steering components such as tie rods.

Transmission

Check the transmission fluid level and condition.

Coolant System

Perform a coolant flush to remove old coolant and ensure the system is functioning correctly.

Engine Components

Check for any error codes in the ECU (Engine Control Unit) and ensure the timing belt/chain is in good condition.
